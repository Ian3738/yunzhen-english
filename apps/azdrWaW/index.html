Be Verb Questions Practice

📚 練習說明 Instructions

將下列陳述句改成問句。記得要把 be 動詞移到句首！

Transform the statements below into questions. Remember to move the be verb to the beginning!

🔑 重要規則 Key Rules:

  • Be 動詞移到句首 (Move be verb to the beginning)
  • 主詞放在 be 動詞後面 (Subject comes after be verb)
  • 句尾加問號 (Add question mark at the end)
  • 首字母大寫 (Capitalize the first letter)

範例 Example:

Statement: He is a teacher.

Question: Is he a teacher?
