Idioms & Phrases 重要片語（共 5 個）

1. as well as
和；除了……以外
⚠️ 重點：A as well as B 當主詞時，動詞的單複數與 A 一致，不看 B！
Each student will be given a textbook as well as a notebook.
→ 動詞用 will be given（與 Each student 一致）
2. for free
免費
副詞片語，修飾動作。同義：free of charge
The videos can be watched for free on TED.com.
3. at present
目前；現在
時間副詞，同義：now / currently，通常搭配現在式動詞使用。
At present, the collection of TED Talk videos is growing week by week.
4. give … a try
嘗試……
用於嘗試新事物或方法。
同義說法：give…a shot / give…a go / give…a whirl
You won't realize how much you can learn unless you give it a try.
5. broaden one's horizons
拓展（某人的）視野
horizons 恆為複數。broaden 也可替換為 expand / widen。
Traveling abroad can really broaden your horizons.

Sentence Patterns 本課句型（共 2 個）

S + keep / leave / find + O + V-ing / p.p. / adj. / prep.
「使／任由／發現……處於某種狀態」
keep、leave、find 是不完全及物動詞，受詞後面要加「受詞補語 (OC)」說明狀態，句子才完整。
① V-ing
現在分詞
主動、進行中的動作
② p.p.
過去分詞
被動、已完成的動作
③ adj.
形容詞
描述狀態
④ prep.
介系詞片語
描述位置、狀態
Don't leave your car running while you fill it with gas.
加油時，別讓車子發動著。（running = V-ing，主動進行）
I was surprised to find the door unlocked when I got home.
我回到家，驚訝地發現門沒上鎖。（unlocked = p.p.，被動）
Please keep the windows open.
請讓窗戶保持開著。（open = adj.，狀態）
Hank woke up and found his dog beside his bed.
Hank 醒來發現狗在床邊。（beside his bed = prep.，位置）
Bill Gates left millions feeling inspired through his TED Talk.
比爾蓋茲透過 TED 演講讓數百萬人受到啟發。（課文例句）
It is/was + adj. + that + S + V...
「……是……的」（虛主詞句型）
that 引導的名詞子句才是真正的主詞。因為太長，所以移到後面，句首用虛主詞 it 代替，避免「頭重腳輕」。
常見形容詞：
true 真實的
clear 明顯的
obvious 明顯的
certain 確定的
possible 可能的
impossible 不可能的
amazing 驚人的
surprising 令人驚訝的
strange 奇怪的
interesting 有趣的
It is interesting that Bill Gates didn't talk about computers.
有趣的是，比爾蓋茲並沒有談論電腦。（課文例句）
It was clear to the organizers that they should invite thinkers from a broader range of fields.
對籌辦者而言，顯然他們應該邀請更廣泛領域的思想家。（課文例句）
It is possible that we will win the championship.
我們有可能會贏得冠軍。
