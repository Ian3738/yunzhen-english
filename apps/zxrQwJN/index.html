📚 Present Tenses Overview

1. Simple Present (簡單現在式)

用途：習慣、事實、時間表

結構：主詞 + V / V-s/es (第三人稱單數)

✓ I study English every day. (習慣)

✓ She works at a bank. (事實)

✓ The train leaves at 9 AM. (時間表)

2. Present Continuous (現在進行式)

用途：正在進行的動作、暫時狀況、計劃

結構：主詞 + am/is/are + V-ing

✓ I am reading a book now. (正在進行)

✓ She is living in Taipei this month. (暫時)

✓ We are meeting tomorrow. (計劃)

3. Present Perfect (現在完成式)

用途：過去到現在的經驗、剛完成的動作、持續狀態

結構：主詞 + have/has + 過去分詞

✓ I have visited Japan three times. (經驗)

✓ She has just finished her homework. (剛完成)

✓ They have lived here for 10 years. (持續)

4. Present Perfect Continuous (現在完成進行式)

用途：強調動作的持續性

結構：主詞 + have/has + been + V-ing

✓ I have been studying for 3 hours. (持續進行)

✓ It has been raining all day. (持續狀態)
